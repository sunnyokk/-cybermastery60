HOW TO USE THIS FILE
01
Read the Theory
Each session opens with core concepts explained in plain language. Read actively — make notes. Annotate terms you don't know immediately.
02
Do the Lab
Every session has a hands-on lab section. Open Kali Linux or TryHackMe alongside this file. Run every command. Passive reading builds knowledge. Active practice builds skill.
03
Answer the Exam Questions
Click each option to reveal the answer and explanation. Do not peek before answering. Honest wrong answers teach more than dishonest right ones.
04
Track Your Progress
Click "Mark Complete" on each day. Your progress is saved to your browser. Return anytime — the file remembers where you were.
05
Review Weekly
Every Sunday, review days completed that week. Re-read the callout boxes. Redo any exam questions you got wrong. Spaced repetition is the engine of retention.
06
Pass the Exam
Book Security+ before Day 60. The act of registering creates commitment. Seat availability fills up. Book early, study hard, sit confident.
