No. Day 1 starts from absolute zero. If you know how to use a computer and have basic curiosity, you have everything required. By Day 10 you will have more foundational knowledge than most people studying for six months on YouTube alone.

A computer with at least 8GB RAM (16GB preferred) to run VirtualBox with Kali Linux and Metasploitable 2. Alternatively, every lab has a TryHackMe browser-based option that requires no local hardware setup at all — just a browser.
